Welcome to the personal webpage of Md. Sifat Ar Salan. I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at Jahangirnagar University, Bangladesh. My research interests lie in the fields of Statistical Modeling, Machine Learning, and Big Data. This webpage provides an overview of my academic background, research, publications, teaching experience, and professional achievements.

About Me

I am currently serving as an Assistant Professor at the Department of Statistics and Data Science, Jahangirnagar University, Dhaka, Bangladesh. I hold a Master of Science in Statistics from the University of Southampton, UK, where I was awarded a Commonwealth Scholarship and graduated with distinction. Additionally, I completed another Master of Science and a Bachelor of Science in Statistics from Jahangirnagar University, securing the top position in my class for both degrees.
